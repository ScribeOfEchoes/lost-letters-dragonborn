Riften – To the One Watching the Vault

The Guild you joined was not the one I found. Ours was a breath away from death, strangled beneath Maven’s heel, drowned in debt and dust.

But the shadow never dies, not truly. It waits. Sometimes that’s all survival is—waiting long enough for the world to forget how it beat you.

If you seek fortune, take it. If you seek silence, make it. But never forget: the price of freedom is knowing when to run, and when to disappear.

—The Last Guildmaster
