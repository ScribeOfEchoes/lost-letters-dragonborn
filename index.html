The Scribe’s Archive

Archivist’s Note:

I am not the author of these letters—only their collector. Scattered throughout Skyrim, left in sanctuaries, ruins, and resting places, they bear the voice of one lost to legend: the Last Dragonborn.

Each letter is a fragment. A glimpse into the burden carried by one who bore too many titles. I have begun compiling what I’ve found below. I believe more remain, buried in the frost of time.
