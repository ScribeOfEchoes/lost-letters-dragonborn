Bards College – To the Ones Who Turn Blood into Ballads

I used to think the sword was louder than the song. That what mattered was who lived and who died, not who remembered.

I was wrong.

Stories survive where kingdoms fall. Songs cross borders even when blades cannot. You will do more for peace than I ever did with my shout.

Keep singing. Even when the halls are empty. Especially then.

—Ysmir, Whose Voice Was Not Just a Weapon
